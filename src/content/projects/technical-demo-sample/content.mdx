## Context

This sample entry exists to preview how a standalone technical demo appears in the showcase. It is useful when the work you want to present is a reusable SDK, an isolated module, or a focused prototype rather than a full product case study.

## What it can represent

- A reusable SDK walkthrough with a controlled demo flow.
- A native module proof of concept that is easier to explain through video than screenshots.
- An internal tooling or technical experiment that should be shown separately from client products.

## Layout behavior

The `technical-demo` kind keeps the same overall showcase structure, adds a dedicated technical-demo badge near the header, and places the demo video in its own panel below the written explanation.
